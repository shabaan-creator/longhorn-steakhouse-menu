Frequently Asked Questions

Does LongHorn Steakhouse have a gluten free menu?
Yes. LongHorn Steakhouse offers gluten-friendly options including certain steaks, salads, and sides. Always inform your server of any dietary requirements before ordering.
Does LongHorn Steakhouse have a lunch menu?
Yes. LongHorn Steakhouse serves lunch daily from 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM at most locations with affordable options including burgers, salads, ribs, and steaks.
What is the best steak at LongHorn Steakhouse?
The Outlaw Ribeye is consistently the most popular steak at LongHorn Steakhouse, known for its bold flavor and generous 20 oz portion. Flo's Filet is best for guests who prefer a tender, buttery texture.
How much does dinner cost at LongHorn Steakhouse?
A typical dinner for one person costs between $20 and $45. The average check per person including an entrée, sides, and a drink is approximately $30 to $35.
Does LongHorn Steakhouse have happy hour?
Some LongHorn Steakhouse locations offer happy hour deals on drinks and appetizers. Check with your nearest location for availability and timing.
Does LongHorn Steakhouse offer senior discounts?
Some locations offer senior discounts. Ask your server or call your local LongHorn Steakhouse to confirm current availability.
Does LongHorn Steakhouse have catering?
Yes. LongHorn Steakhouse provides catering services for events and gatherings. Contact your nearest location directly for catering menu options and pricing.
